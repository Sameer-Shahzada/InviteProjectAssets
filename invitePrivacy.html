Problem Statement

In rural as well as in urban areas there are numbers of micro as well as small business exists such as - (Kirana store, repairing shop, electronic shop, medical stores, Doctors clinic and more various categories …...etc), these business are not reachable effectively by the consumer or there may be subtle un-awareness such as - shop address, contact number and service provided, and many more things.

Solution

A Shop creation interface for the business/shop owner in order to create and register their shop. After completed the register process the shop is being created and available for the advertisement on another panel/page.

Shop Category Interface, every shop is being categorised according to their domain of operation. User Authentication, a user may or may not be shop holder so, there is need to authenticate the user in order to provide the service.
